About Save'nServe

Bridging the gap between food waste and hunger

Save'nServe is a foundation dedicated to reducing food waste while fighting hunger in Tunisia. We partner with restaurants, hotels, and food establishments to rescue surplus food that would otherwise go to waste.

Our mission is simple: collect quality, unused food and distribute it to families and individuals in need across Tunisia's most vulnerable communities.

Every day, we work tirelessly to ensure that no good food goes to waste while no one goes hungry. Through our network of dedicated volunteers and partners, we create a sustainable cycle of giving that benefits both the environment and those in need.
